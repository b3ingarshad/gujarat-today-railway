About Us

Our very own “Gujarat Today” is a daily published with fearless, impartial, selfless, competent, helpful, collaborative, articulate, cultured talent.

  • First regional language newspaper started by Muslims of Gujarat.
  • On July 29, 1991, the first edition of “Gujarat Today” daily was released.
  • An unbiased and neutral newspaper without obscenity and yellow journalism, whose policy is based on the principles of Islam.
  • “Gujarat Today” is the only such newspaper in which you will not see ugly pictures or text, allowing sisters and children to read without hesitation.
  • Started by Lokhit Prakashan Public Trust, “Gujarat Today” has contributed significantly in the social and educational fields.
  • The head office of “Gujarat Today” is in Ahmedabad, where the entire process from creating news to printing the paper is done in one place.
  • Offices of “Gujarat Today” are also located in Baroda, Bharuch, Surat, with honorary reporters at the district level in Palanpur, Himmatnagar, Patan, Dahod, Godhra, Mehsana, Anand, Junagadh, Rajkot, Bhavnagar, Jamnagar, Bhuj, and other locations.
